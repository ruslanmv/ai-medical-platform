# Internal Medicine Department Introduction

## Main Divisions
   - Cardiology
   - Gastroenterology
   - Pulmonology
   - Endocrinology
   - Nephrology

## Details of Each Division

### Cardiology
#### Introduction:
Cardiology focuses on heart diseases and disorders.

#### Common Conditions Treated:
- Hypertension
- Coronary artery disease
- Heart failure

#### Treatment Methods:
- Medication management
- Cardiac rehabilitation
- Lifestyle modification

### Gastroenterology
#### Introduction:
Gastroenterology deals with digestive system diseases.

#### Common Conditions Treated:
- Acid reflux
- Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)
- Liver disease

#### Treatment Methods:
- Endoscopic procedures
- Dietary management

### Pulmonology
#### Introduction:
Pulmonology specializes in lung and respiratory diseases.

#### Common Conditions Treated:
- Asthma
- Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
- Pneumonia

#### Treatment Methods:
- Bronchodilators
- Oxygen therapy

### Endocrinology
#### Introduction:
Endocrinology deals with hormone-related disorders.

#### Common Conditions Treated:
- Diabetes
- Thyroid disorders
- Osteoporosis

#### Treatment Methods:
- Hormone therapy
- Lifestyle changes

### Nephrology
#### Introduction:
Nephrology focuses on kidney diseases.

#### Common Conditions Treated:
- Chronic kidney disease
- Kidney stones

#### Treatment Methods:
- Dialysis
- Kidney transplantation
